Campus Life notified EPD of a strong odor of marijuana coming from a room at the location. Officers arrived on the scene and entered the room. They noticed a glass pipe andmarijuana ashes in plain view. The individual who lived in the room was not cited because there was a lack of any substance other than the residue.
