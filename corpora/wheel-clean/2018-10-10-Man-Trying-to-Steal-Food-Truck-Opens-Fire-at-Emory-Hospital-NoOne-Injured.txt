A man trying to steal a food truck from an Emory University Hospital (EUH) loading dock opened fire at about 6 a.m. on Wednesday. No one was injured, according to Emory Police Department (EPD).The suspect, a Forest Park, Ga., resident, was taken into police custody and has been charged with carrying a weapon within a school zone as a non-license holder, aggravated assault, possession of firearm during attempt to commit certain felonies and more.EPD and Atlanta Police Department (APD) arrived on the scene.
